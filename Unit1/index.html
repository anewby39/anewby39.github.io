Macoroni and Cheese

  1. Ingredients:

    • Milk
    • Cheese Powder
    • Macoroni Noodles
    • Water
    • Butter

  2. Steps:

    • Boil Noodles
    • Strain noodles to get them out of water
    • Put noodles back in pot
    • Mix with 1/4 cup of milk and hald stick of butter
    • Mix with cheese sauce
    • Enjoy!
